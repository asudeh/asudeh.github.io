	<div class="tab-pane active" id="MCAbstract" role="tabpanel" aria-labelledby="MCA-tab">
		Data-driven technologies are only as good as the data they work with.
		On the other hand, data scientists have often limited control on how the data is collected.
		Failing to contain adequate number of instances from minority (sub)groups, known as population bias, is a major reason for model unfairness and disparate performance across different groups.
		<br>
		In this paper, we demonstrate \system, a system for investigating population bias over the intersection of multiple attributes.
		We use the concept of {\em coverage} for identifying intersectional subgroups with inadequate representation in the dataset.
		\system\ is a web application with an interactive visual interface that allows data scientists to explore the dataset and identify subgroups with poor {\em coverage}.
