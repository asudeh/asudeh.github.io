Social Activities - UTA PACS

 UTA - Persian Academic and Cultural Society (PACS) is a non-profit, non-political, non-religious organization established for all people interested in Iran. I started my activities there as soon as I joined UTA (Spring 2012).
I served as the President of PACS at Fall 2012 and Spring 2013. One of our main achievements in that year was the development of the comprehensive web site of PACS, which is the result of several months effort of the content collection and development teams (10 students).
At Fall 2013 and Spring 2014, I was the Vice President of PACS. The powerful team of PACS officers in that year resulted in winning the "Best Example of Teamwork by a Student Organization of the 2013 - 2014" Award, among about 300 student organizations. This was the first time, PACS won such an award.
