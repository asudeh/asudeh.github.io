Social Activities - UTA PACS

 UTA PACS (Persian Academic and Cultural Society) is a non-profit, non-political, non-religious student organization at the University of Texas at Arlington.
Abolfazl served as the President of PACS in academic year 2012-2013. One of their main achievements in that year was the development of the comprehensive web site of PACS, which is the result of several months effort of the content collection and development teams (10 students).
He also served as the Vice President of PACS in academic year 2013-2014. They received the "Best Example of Teamwork by a Student Organization of the 2013 - 2014" Award, among about 300 student organizations. This was the only time, PACS won such an award.
