Short Bio

I am a Research Fellow, and a member of the Database Research Group, at the Computer Science and Engineering department of the University of Michigan, working with Dr. H V Jagadish. Here at U-M, I lead Mithra, our project on Responsible Data Management and Data Ethics. I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Arlington, where I was supervised by Dr. Gautam Das as a member of Database Exploration Lab (DBXLAB). I finished my academic educations with the Outstanding PhD Student Award.

My research interests include Responsible Data Science and Data Ethics, Big Data Management and Analysis, Data Mining, Machine Learning, Ranking and Top-k Query Processing, Compact Maxima Representatives, Algorithm Design, Combinatorial Geometry, and Web Data Retrieval. I have a strong publication record in SIGMOD and VLDB in the past few years, including recognitions such as invitation to the "Best of VLDB". My erdös number is 3.
I was a research intern at Microsoft Research, DMX group, in Summer 2016. In summer 2014, I was a software engineering intern at Microsoft. I also have the experience of teaching different CS courses.
