ABOUT ME

Hi. My name is Abolfazl (Ab) Asudeh. I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington. I am very fortunate to have Dr. Gautam Das as my Ph.D. advisor, to be a member of DBXLAB, and to have the chance of collaborating with Dr. Gergely Zaruba, Dr. Nan Zhang, and Dr. Changkai Li . I received my M.Sc. in Computer Engineering - Software  from the Computer Engineering department at Sharif University of Technology under the supervision of Dr. Ali Movaghar in 2008. Before joining UTA, I had the chance of teaching the computer science courses at several universities of Iran, from 2008 to 2011.
I have a broad area of research interests, including Big Data Exploration, Graph Mining, and Crowdsourcing and Human Computation.
