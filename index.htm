Short Bio

I (Abolfazl Asudeh) am a Research Fellow, and a member of the Database Research Group, at the Computer Science and Engineering department of the University of Michigan, working with Dr. H V Jagadish. I received my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Arlington, where I was supervised by Dr. Gautam Das as a member of Database Exploration Lab (DBXLAB). I finished my academic educations with the Outstanding PhD Student Award.
My research interests include Responsible Data Management and Data Ethics, Ranking Queries and Compact Representatives, Data Mining, Algorithm Design, Web Databases, Information Retrieval, Graph Analytics, Combinatorial Geometry, Machine Learning, and Crowdsourcing. Most of my publications are in VLDB and SIGMOD. My erdös number is 3.
I was a research intern at Microsoft Research, DMX group in Summer 2016. In summer 2014, I was a software engineering intern at Microsoft. I also have the experience of teaching different CS courses.
