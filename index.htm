ABOUT ME

Hi. My name is Abolfazl (Ab) Asudeh. I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington. I am very fortunate to have Dr. Gautam Das as my Ph.D. advisor and to be a member of DBXLAB. My research interests are around database and data mining algorithms, including Top-k and Reranking Queries/Indices, Deep Web, and Graph Mining. I received my M.Sc. in Computer Engineering - Software from the Computer Engineering department at Sharif University of Technology in 2008.
