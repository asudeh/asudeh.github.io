Matteo Corain is a Computer Science graduate from University of Illinois at Chicago and Politecnico di Torino (Italy), with a fond interest in data science and data mining. He obtained his Master of Science degree with full marks in 2020; his thesis work (“A density-based method for scalable outlier detection in large datasets”) was jointly advised by prof. Asudeh and prof. Garza (Politecnico di Torino). Previously, he obtained a BS degree with full marks in the same field from Politecnico di Torino (2018).
